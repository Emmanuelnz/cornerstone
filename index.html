About

Who We Are

Cornerstone Inheritance House is a community outreach hub supplying spiritual, economical and emotional support to our neighbors in Kosciusko county in Indiana. We believe that we are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus to our community.

The Cornerstone Inheritance House is a place where worship, prayer and acts of service bring life & resources to hurting people by showing the love and character of Christ.
